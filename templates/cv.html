James Waples

Experienced Rust developer

Edinburgh, Scotland

Hi, I'm James, a Rust, electronics and CNC enthusiast. I've been programming for about 20 years since I was 10 years old, starting with 8 bit assembler, Visual Basic and C. I started my professional career at 18 with a PHP web development role and slowly moved towards the JavaScript/Typescript ecosystem as it grew around the popularity of libraries like React. My work now revolves around the wonderful Rust programming language, gladly using it both professionally and in open source to write high quality code for various environments.

I'm motivated by engaging software problems, challenging optimisations, interesting performance debugging and making a polished product for people to use. I strongly believe we work best in a team, each of us adding to something greater than the sum of parts.

I also have a strong interest in electronics. It was a quick discovery after computers and has become a long running hobby, most recently using Rust on STM32 microcontrollers.

Melding electronics and programming lead me to discover the expensive yet fascinating world of DIY CNC machines, myself having built at least 5 of improving capabilities (and heft) over time. It's a great escape from the keyboard to step into the workshop and make something in the physical world.
